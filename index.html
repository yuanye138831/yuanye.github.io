About me

I am a research associate of Chongqing Green Intelligent Technology Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I have published over 24 SCI/EI papers, including top journals and conferences like IEEE T. KDE, CYB, WWW, and ECAI in the computer field, applied 11 national invention patents with 5 authorized, won the First Prize of Wu Wenjun AI Science and Technology Progress Award (11/15) and the First Prize of Chongqing Science and Technology Progress Award (11/15). Now i am a reviewer of IEEE/CAA Journal of Automatica Sinica, IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man and Cybernetics: Systems, and Neurocomputing.

Research interest: Data mining, Machine learning, Recommendation system
